ApolloKit is a **SaaS live-ops infrastructure platform** for games and apps. It transforms the common "every team rewrites this" backend work — economy, live ops, social, analytics — into **configurable, ready-to-use modules**, with built-in AI agents that let operations teams configure everything in plain language without waiting on engineering.

## Who Is It For?

| Role | What They Get |
|------|--------------|
| **Decision-makers / Executives** | Lower recurring engineering costs, faster time-to-market for new features, shared infrastructure across all products |
| **Product Managers** | Clear module boundaries, rapid live-ops iteration, no need to write specs and wait for sprints |
| **Operations Teams** | Self-serve dashboard, AI-assisted configuration, ship campaigns without engineering involvement |
| **Engineering Teams** | Integrate the SDK once; stop rebuilding the same infrastructure for every product |
