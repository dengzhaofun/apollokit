The collection module implements "Pokédex / weapon compendium / monster encyclopedia" style unlock-progress gameplay: admins configure an album, players unlock entries through various activities, and milestone thresholds automatically trigger rewards.

## What problem does it solve?

"Collect everything" is one of the strongest long-term retention hooks in games, but the system has to be rebuilt every time:

- How to track each player's unlock state for each entry (high data volume)
- Does the milestone fire on "complete the whole album" or "any 5 categories"?
- Should players actively claim rewards, or should they auto-grant? How to prevent duplicates?

The collection module encapsulates all of these rules. Configure an album and it's ready to use.

## Typical use cases

**Scenario 1: Weapon compendium**
100 weapons — picking up a weapon for the first time unlocks its entry. Collect 10 for a small reward, 50 for a large reward, all 100 for the ultimate skin.

**Scenario 2: Monster encyclopedia**
Defeating a monster for the first time unlocks its entry. Grouped by region — collecting all monsters in a region triggers a region milestone reward.

**Scenario 3: Event-limited album**
A special collection album only available during an event. Archived when the event ends. Players collect as much as possible during the event window, with the final snapshot preserved.

**Scenario 4: Achievement-driven collection**
"First group session," "first purchase," "first 3-star clear" — one-time achievements managed uniformly through a collection album, rather than scattered across individual modules.

## Core capabilities

- **Group management**: entries within an album can be categorized; milestones can target the full album or specific groups
- **Multiple milestone triggers**: full album progress / group progress / specific entry unlock — flexibly configured
- **Auto-grant**: milestones support `autoClaim` — rewards are delivered automatically on unlock, no player action needed
- **Idempotent unlocking**: unlocking the same entry multiple times only records once — safe to retry

## Who is it for?

| Role | What they get |
|------|--------------|
| Decision-makers | Collection systems are one of the lowest-cost long-term retention mechanisms |
| Product managers | Flexible collection rules and milestone rewards scaled to any content volume |
| Operations team | View completion rate data — useful for planning future content |
| Engineering team | Call one unlock endpoint when the player gets new content; everything else is automatic |
